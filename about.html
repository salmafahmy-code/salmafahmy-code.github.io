About Homes4All

Homes4All began as a grassroots movement of students, tenants, and advocates who saw firsthand how rising housing costs were reshaping their city. Today, we're a community-driven NGO working toward long-term solutions for housing affordability in Vancouver.

What We Do

  • Advocate for evidence-based housing policies.
  • Share data and resources with renters, students, and families.
  • Partner with local organizations to amplify awareness.

Our Vision

We imagine a future where every Vancouverite — regardless of income — has access to safe, stable, and affordable housing. Our work focuses on community support, transparency, and empowerment.
