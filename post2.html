Community Land Trusts: A Path Forward

Community Land Trusts (CLTs) offer a promising alternative to speculative real estate markets. By placing land in community stewardship, CLTs ensure that housing remains affordable for generations to come.

In Vancouver, several pilot projects have demonstrated the power of collective ownership models. Through advocacy and policy reform, Homes4All supports the growth of CLTs to create lasting affordability and equity in housing.
