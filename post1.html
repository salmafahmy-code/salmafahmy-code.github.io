Rising Rent and Working-Class Vancouverites

Over the last decade, Vancouver has seen rent prices rise at nearly twice the rate of average income growth. Essential workers — nurses, retail staff, and service providers — now face impossible choices between long commutes, cramped conditions, or leaving the city entirely.

Housing affordability is not just an economic issue — it's a community one. When those who sustain a city can no longer live in it, the entire urban fabric begins to unravel. At Homes4All, we advocate for policies that prioritize equitable housing development and rent stabilization to keep Vancouver livable for all.
